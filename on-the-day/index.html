On the day

The big day is 12th September, we hope you find everything that you need here to celebrate the day with us. If you have any questions, feel free to get in touch at sam.jbmason+wedding@gmail.com.

  • 2:30pm Everyone is welcome to join Sam for drinks at The Taproom (163 Upper Street) for some dutch courage
  • 3:30pm Guests arrive at Islington Town Hall (Upper Street, N1 2UD) for the ceremony
  • 4pm Ceremony begins
  • 4:30pm WE'RE MARRIED!
  • 5pm Bus leaves Town Hall for guests going onto the wedding breakfast at 6 St Chads Place (Kings Cross, WC1X 9HH)
  • 5:45pm Wedding breakfast
  • 8:30pm Evening guests arrive for the cake cutting, first dance and partying!
  • 1am Bar closes & Carriages
  • Sunday 13th midday Recovery lunch at The Old Queens Head (44 Essex Rd, N1 8LN)

Guests attending the wedding breakfast should hop on the bus by 5pm outside the Town Hall. You will enjoy a sightseeing detour and arrive at the reception venue at 5:45pm.

All other guests can feel free to try one of the many awesome bars and restaurants around Islington until the evening celebrations begins at 8:30pm. We have included some options below:

If you're sticking around Upper Street:

  • Be At One - Bang for your buck
  • Ziloufs - Cool bar to grab a drink and some good food
  • Le Mercure Deuxième - Fantastic and affordable French cuisine
  • Bill’s - Big selection of yummy food and Sasha's favourite elderflower fizz

If you're heading closer to Kings Cross:

  • Honest Burger - If you're not afraid of grease, great burgers and rosemary fries
  • Dishoom - Amazing modern take on indian food
  • Caravan - Hipster food for hipster people
