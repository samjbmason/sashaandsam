The Story So Far

We first met each other in our work lift after Sam accepted a job in London. A 6-month friendship bloomed from a mutual love for music, cigarettes and comedy shows. After going to see the Phenomenal Handclap Band for the second time in 1 week (and a bit of intervention from Ed Holmes and Camilla McCusker nee de Caires) we realised we both held huge crushes on each other. Overnight our friendship transformed into a relationship.

Sam is 3 years Sasha’s junior and our colleagues welcomed the news with this. Needless to say we’ve always kept a bit of humour and silliness around.

5 years on, there has been 1 marriage proposal, 1 surrogate child in the form of a cat called Rufus, 6 new jobs, 1 mortgage and 118 floorboards laid with a lot of help from family. We look forward to becoming a kickass husband and wife team.
